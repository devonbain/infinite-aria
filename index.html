About

Inspired by "Aria" by John Cage, this piece uses Cage's style of graphic notation combined with stochastic procedures (i.e. Math.random()) to generate each squiggly line and accompanying text. The length of the piece is left to the performer; it can theoretically be performed forever, as there are nearly infinite combinations of colors, texts, and line shapes. Texts come from various opera and oratorio libretti which are in the public domain.
